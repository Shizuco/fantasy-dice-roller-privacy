Privacy Policy

Fantasy Dice Roller ("the App") is designed to respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

The App does not require an account or registration.

We do not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Files
  • Microphone
  • Camera
  • Location

The App works completely offline except when displaying advertisements, processing purchases, or communicating with Google Play services.

Local Storage

The following information may be stored locally on your device:

  • Roll history
  • Dice roll statistics
  • Theme preference
  • Sound preference
  • Vibration preference
  • Premium purchase status
  • Application settings
  • Internal counters used to determine when advertisements are shown
  • Internal counters used to determine when the app review dialog may appear

This information remains stored only on your device and is never transmitted to the developer.

Advertising

The free version of the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google may automatically collect information necessary to provide, measure, and improve advertising services, depending on your region and consent settings.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase that permanently removes advertisements.

Payments are securely processed through Google Play Billing.

The developer never receives your payment information or billing credentials.

Google Play In-App Review

The App may occasionally display the official Google Play review dialog.

Whether the review prompt has already been shown is stored only on your device.

The developer never receives the content of your review.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following Google services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play In-App Review

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Fantasy Dice Roller does not transmit your roll history, statistics, or settings to any external server.

All gameplay-related information remains stored exclusively on your own device.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected on this page together with an updated Last updated date.
